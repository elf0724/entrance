臺　南　古　蹟　照　片　輯

　　臺南市，位於臺灣西南平野，是臺灣開發最早的城市，擁有全島最豐富的人文資源，也是我出生長大的地方，臺南市的古蹟之多，位居全臺灣各縣市之冠，在國家核定的古蹟中，臺南市共有五十幾處之多，由於數目眾多，古蹟的密度很高，因此古蹟與臺南人的生活是密不可分的，記得小時候常在赤崁樓裏跑來跑去，高中聯考前學校老師也會帶著大家一起去孔廟祭拜孔子，而南門城就緊臨著學校後面，大天后宮每年農曆三月的迓媽祖更是兒時最喜歡的廟會迎熱閙活動、、、諸如此類的生活細節，在從前並不覺得有什麼特別，日子就這樣日復一日地流過，我也從國小、國中、高中一路唸上來，到了上大學之後，便離開故鄉到臺北來，就這麼一待十幾年。由於喜歡臺北的生活便利、資訊流通、文化多元、風景玩樂、、、也喜歡臺南的美食小吃、溫暖氣候、民情敦厚、步調悠閒、、、等，所以我常在兩個城市間往返穿梭，感受兩個城市各自獨有的魅力。

　　2006年初，於拍賣網站上買了部二手數位相機，對於底片相機時代拍照經常被其他人嫌棄的我來說，數位相機是很棒的東西，有了它，拍照時就可以不必擔心浪費底片而不斷練習下去，從此打開了我對學習攝影的興趣；由於喜歡到處遊山玩水，所以最常拍的主題是「風景」，臺北近郊有許多美麗的風景，只是多數人並不清楚，實在可惜，至於每次回臺南最常拍的主題則是「古蹟」，而且因為距離都很近，所以很容易到達，同一個地方也可以反覆不停地去拍，隨著拍照經驗一點一滴地累積，發現臺南市擁有這麼多古蹟，對於喜歡攝影的人來說，是件很幸福的事，攝影須要「主題」才會吸引人，在臺南拍古蹟的每張照片，都因為有著明確的主題而更容易成為動人的作品，這個網站便是希望把目前所拍下的臺南市古蹟美麗影像呈現出來，讓更多人可以認識臺南府城古都之美，也歡迎所有喜歡這些照片的人，可以到臺南走走，實地拜訪一下這些珍貴的名勝古蹟，相信臺南優美的傳統文化和敦厚的民情，您一定會喜歡。

MY PHOTO COLLECTION OF TAINAN HISTORIC SITE
ALL BY Z.H.CHEN

    I was born and grown up in Tainan City which located in the southwestern plain of Taiwan with abundant human and literary resources. There are many historic sites located in Tainan City and it's the city that owns the most historic sites in the Taiwan region. In the historic sites designated by authority, over fifty sites located in Tainan City, so the density of historic sites is very high and inseparable with people's life in Tainan. When I was a child, I usually activate around the Chihkan Tower. My junior high school teacher brought us to Tainan Confucius Temple to do obeisance before senior high school entrance examination. The Historic City South Gate is near by the back door of my junior high school. The Mazu festivities of Official Mazu Temple in every third month of the lunar calendar is my most favorite temple fair....... Such as these, I didn't feel anything special in the past and life flowed day after day. I finished my elementary school, junior high school, senior high school in Tainan City. And then I must leave my hometown to Taipei to study at university. I've been Taipei City more than ten years. The convenience living, circulating information, diverse culture, beautiful scenery and joyful life are what I like with Taipei. The delicacies food & snack, warm weather, people live and feel is honest and sincere, path and pace is leisurely and carefree are what I like with Tainan. Therefore, I often come and go between these two cities to immerse myself in the different city charm.

  In early 2006,I bought a digital camera on Yahoo bid.tw. I was abandoned when used a film camera, but I could take photos in my way and not to worry about the strike-rate with a digital camera. It’s a wonderful tool for me and interests me in photography. I usually travel high and low and enjoy the sights of mountains and rivers, so my favorite topic of photograph is "scenery". I think it’s a pity that there’re so much beautiful scenery in the environs of Taipei but most people don't know where it is. On the other hand, my favorite topic of photograph in Tainan is "historic site". The distance between historic sites is very close and easy to arrive, so I often take photos in the same site once and once again. As my experience of photograph grows up, I found it so lucky for people who love photography that there're so many historic sites located in Tainan City. Photograph need "explicit topic" to be attracted. Every photo of historic sites in Tainan could become a attracted artistry because of its definitely topic. I'd like to build up this website to show what I've photographed with the historic sites of Tainan City and let more people know the beauty of Tainan as a traditional city. I wish you'll like these photos. You're welcome to visit Tainan City and the historic sites. I think the graceful traditional culture and the honest & sincere people will make you feel very joyful.
