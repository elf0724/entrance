Project4 Anping Fish Market

  There is an "Anping Fish Market" near by the sea in Anping, Tainan City, Taiwan. It's different from the common tourism fish market opening in daytime. It's a wholesale fish market where the fishing boats unload their fishing attains directly and opens from am12:00~6:00 every day. These hard-working people at night witness the diligent part of Taiwanese day after day.

  To avoid alerting the fish market staff, I take my lightweight OM-4Ti film camera as my tool to shoot pictures here. And I use black-white film - "Kodak Tri-X400" & color negative movie film - "Kodak 500T" which is also a tungsten film. It could present beautiful color better than the daylight film in the fish market which uses the bulbs as main lighting.

  "Anping Fish Market" is a good theme for photography. It's very satisfied with the photos shot in two nights. I think it could be continued to shot from now on.
